HOW TO PLAY

You are an AI-powered farming drone. Your mission: protect crops, eliminate pests, and learn agri-tech!

MOVE with WASD or Arrow Keys

ATTACK press Q to fire laser at nearest enemy

PLANT press E over green plots to plant seeds

SCAN press R to scan area for data (+XP)

DODGE Space to roll and avoid damage

SKILLS keys 1-4 for special abilities

Defeat waves of pests and diseases. After each wave you get an Agri-Tech quiz — correct answers give BONUS rewards! Answer to learn about precision farming, sensors, GPS, water management and more.

Every 5 waves — fight a BOSS! Watch out for the Blight Titan and Swarm Queen!
