📖 How to Play

🎯 Goal: Answer farming questions correctly to build your farm! Each correct answer grows your crops, earns gold, and builds reputation.

⏱ Timer: Answer before time runs out! Wrong answers or timeouts cost you a life and damage your farm.

🔥 Combo: Chain correct answers for multiplied rewards! Combo x3+ triggers special bonuses to your farm stats.

📊 Farm Stats: Your Crops, Water, Health, and Reputation change based on your decisions. Keep them balanced to succeed!

🏆 Win: Complete all rounds or reach maximum farm reputation to become a Harvest Tycoon!
